Project test bench

The stability test-bench desk, located in my office at Wyplay. The PC is the "streamer" (DVB injector). Only one frequency (so few xhannels) are actually streamed. The four STBs are zapping between those channels each 10s. This helped to track rare race conditions, memory leaks, or simply test the current version against the Key Performance Indicators.
